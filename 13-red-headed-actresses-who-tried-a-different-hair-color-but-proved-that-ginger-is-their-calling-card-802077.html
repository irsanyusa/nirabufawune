13 Red-Headed Actresses Who Tried a Different Hair Color but Proved That Ginger Is Their Calling Car

June 27, 2024
blog

Only about 1-2% of the population of the planet has natural red hair which is why this hair color is really noticeable and it makes people unique. In Hollywood, there are actresses with red hair and they are incredibly charming.

We at Bright Side decided to find out what some famous red-headed women look like with other hair colors and we suddenly realized that they look way better with red hair, even if it’s not their natural color.

Emma Stone

This actress is one of the most famous red-headed actresses in Hollywood. But her natural hair color is actually blonde and she sometimes goes back to it. Emma says that she feels like a redhead not like a blonde.

Evan Rachel Wood

Here’s another natural blonde who loves experimenting with her hair color. Evan said in an interview that bright red is the only color she likes her hair to be.

Amy Adams

It seems that this redheaded actress has never been seen with different hair but in reality, she is blonde. At the age of 27, she was working on a film with another blonde actress and this happened, “I’m in a room with her, and they said, ‘Someone’s going to have to change their hair color.’ It really changed things up. People began to see me in a different way, for different roles.”

Marcia Cross

The star of Desperate Housewives is famous for her bright red hair. And it’s her natural color that she looks much better in than blonde hair.

Bryce Dallas Howard

Bryce is the daughter of the famous director Ron Howard who is also a ginger. So, her red hair is her natural color and she looks great with it.

Nicole Kidman

In her earlier projects, you can see her natural hair color: bright curly red hair. Now, she often has blonde hair, and wears wigs in all sorts of different colors. Do you think she looks good with dark hair?

Kate Mara

Kate is actually red-headed but she likes experimenting with hair color and length. It seems that she looks great with any hair color. What do you think?

Karen Gillan

The star of Jumanji is actually red-headed. She sometimes wears wigs in different colors but she admits that she loves red the most.

Lily Cole

This British model and actress is totally okay when it comes to experimenting with her hair color. But we think that her natural red hair highlights her unusual appearance.

Lindsay Lohan

She has been on the big screen since she was a child. So everyone has seen her natural red hair. For a long time, she liked blonde but she’s recently returned to red hair making it a bit brighter than her natural shade.

Jessica Chastain

Jessica Chastain’s red hair is her natural color. For some roles, she uses wigs — she doesn’t like dying her hair because she thinks that red makes her stand out.

Isla Fisher

One of the most famous redheads in Hollywood seems to have never changed her hair color. But in 2019, she decided to shock her fans when she appeared as a blonde in her Instagram post. Obviously, it was just a wig because just several days later, Isla appeared at an event with her usual red hair.

Bonnie Wright

Bonnie Wright has the same hair color as her famous character Ginny Weasley. Sometimes, she experiments with dying her hair but we love her red hair so much more.

What’s your natural hair color? Have you ever dyed your hair red?
