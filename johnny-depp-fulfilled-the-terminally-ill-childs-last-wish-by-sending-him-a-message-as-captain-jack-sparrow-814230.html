Johnny Depp Dresses as Captain Jack Sparrow for the Terminally Ill Child Despite His Promise Never G

May 20, 2024
blog

Johnny Depp has recently acknowledged that he would never rejoin Disney after being dismissed from his portrayal of Captain Jack Sparrow. Nonetheless, this hasn’t stopped the actor from using the image of the “greatest pirate we’ve ever seen” to improve the lives of others.

Johnny Depp supported the ill child dressed as Jack Sparrow.

One of the most heartwarming examples of the close bond Johnny Depp shares with his fans is demonstrated through his interaction with Kori Stovell, an 11-year-old child suffering from a terminal illness and born with an undisclosed heart condition.

In December, with the help of Make-a-Wish, Depp took a break from his busy schedule to connect with Stovell via video conference. Being an ardent fan of Depp’s portrayal of Captain Jack Sparrow in the Pirates of the Caribbean franchise, Stovell, who had undergone two heart transplants, expressed his wish to meet Depp and asked the renowned actor to subscribe to his YouTube channel, “Kraken the Box.” During their virtual meeting, Depp affectionately dubbed Stovell “Captain Kori” and promised to become a follower of the young child’s YouTube channel.

Kori was a huge fan of Jack Sparrow.

So after meeting with Johnny Depp online, the boy shared a special video featuring the beloved Hollywood star in his cherished Pirates character. In the footage, Depp’s Captain Jack can be heard saying, “So, Capt. Kori, terribly sorry I missed out on this. Meant to say it, forgot to say it, didn’t say it, gonna say it now. Saying it, I’ll say it.

I hear tell of something they speak about in the ages of now called the YouTube channel, which I don’t understand, but why not?...

I think it will make for a wonderful entertainment expertise momentary lapse of togetherness altogether in one space, but far apart, but then, at the same time, very close. Curious, enduring and strange.”

The source says Kori was a huge fan of Jack Sparrow, frequently watching the Pirates of the Caribbean movies “during his multiple heart surgeries and subsequent recovery periods.”

The actor also paid tribute when Kori passed.

Tragically, one magazine reported the devastating news of “Captain Kori’s” passing, as both of his attempted heart transplants proved unsuccessful. In response to this heartbreaking update, Johnny Depp expressed his condolences and paid tribute to Kori on social media. Declaring himself as Kori’s biggest fan, Depp took to Instagram and shared a heartfelt message that undoubtedly honors the memory of “Captain Kori”:

“Sail on, my fellow captain!! You honored us with your life. You humbled and amazed us with the courage and strength to be able to smile through it all. You’ve shown us how to stay ahead of the curve with grace, humor, incomprehensible understanding, and unparalleled dignity. You are a warrior, mate...we will meet again on the highest of seas...all my love and respect to you and your family always!!! X JD. ”

Although many people remain fans of Captain Jack Sparrow, it is confirmed that Johnny Depp has completely cut ties with Disney. The reason for this decision stems from his deep resentment towards the studio, as they terminated his contract abruptly during his legal dispute with his ex-wife, Amber Heard.
