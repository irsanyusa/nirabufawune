America’s Got Talent: All-Stars returned on Monday for the last night of performances before the Finals, as 10 more acts took the stage. All the Golden Buzzers had been used up, meaning it was up to the Superfans to choose one more act to go to the Finals. That spot ultimately went to singer Kodi Lee.

Kodi Lee Is Going to the AGT: All-Stars Finals

Kodi Lee’s Golden Buzzer audition for Season 14 took the internet by storm, and he ended up winning the season. The singer returned to the AGT stage during Monday night’s show to deliver another stunning performance.

He performed “Biblical” by Calum Scott while playing the piano. The judges and the audience gave Kodi a standing ovation, and the crowd chanted his name. Judge Heidi Klum told Kodi that her “heart doubles in size when I hear you sing.”

Kodi was up against another AGT winner during Monday’s show. Season 15 poet and fellow Golden Buzzer recipient Brandon Leake took the stage to share the powerful story about never judging a book by its cover. Simon Cowell called his performance “absolutely brilliant.”

At the end of the night, both Brandon and Kodi were part of the Top 3 acts according to the Superfans, along with Season 16 comedian Josh Blue. Josh came in third place, and Kodi was announced as the single act going through to the AGT: All-Stars Finals.

Infamous Contestant Sethward Returns as a Goat

The final performance of the night came from infamous contestant Sethward. He shared before his appearance that he’s been on AGT more times than any other contestant. In the past, he’s dressed as a worm and a giraffe.

This time, Sethward declared himself the GOAT by, well, dressing as one. He fell down the stairs and ate Cowell’s tangerine before breaking one of his legs. Cowell hated the performance so much, he hit his fellow judges’ red buzzers.

Other memorable performances from Monday night’s show came from Season 15 singer Daneliya Tuleshova, who performed “Arcade” by Duncan Laurence, as well as Season 14 magician Eric Chien, who delivered a mesmerizing close-up magic routine.
