Guests at the funeral of Nollywood actress, Cynthia Okereke, participated in a traditional Igbo practice.

Funeral guests launch search for deceased actress

Dressed in black attire, the guests were observed running around as a way to honour and pay tribute to the late actress.

Man plans his funeral ahead of his death

Meanwhile, Legit.ng previously reported that a 70-year-old man, Leo, dug his grave in preparation for his death. The man said he had to take that step because the poor members of his community found it financially hard to bury people who recently died.
