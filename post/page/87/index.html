The survey is conducted monthly among a representative sample of 2,200 U.S. adults. The first run took place in September 2020, with additional questions added in subsequent versions. Data is collected during the first week of each month, with all questions pertaining to the previous month. Morning Consult calculates consumer spending estimates by taking the trimmed mean of open-ended numerical response data.
