Best Slipcovered Sofas: 10 Stylish Options for Any Budget

June 02, 2024
blog

Whether you’re going for the farmhouse vibe or want a durable option in case of spills, a slipcovered sofa is a fantastic choice for style and functionality. A slipcovered sofa is simply a sofa with a removable, washable cover (with some even machine-washable) and nowadays they are as pretty as they are practical.

But not all slipcover sofas look like the stark white ones plastered all over Instagram either. There’s a myriad of choices available no matter your favorite design aesthetic, budget, or fabric needs. We’ve sifted through the choices and put together this curated list of our favorite and best slipcovered sofas, so you can find the perfect fit for your home.

What to look for in a slipcovered sofa

Like shopping for the best mattresses or really any major furniture purchase in your home, you should carefully consider which features and options are most important for your household and what will be the most functional. For slipcovered sofas, we recommend you choose one based on the following.

  • Size and shape: Carefully measure and make sure the sofa fits in your room. No matter how good it looks, if it doesn’t fit, it won’t function properly. The great thing about slipcovered sofas is they’re available in a variety of shapes and sizes, so you can find one that’ll fit your space perfectly.
  • Fabric choice: Look for ones matching your lifestyle, whether it’s stain-resistant, easy to clean, machine washable (versus dry clean only) and make the selection based on this.
  • Maintenance: Double check the slipcover can be maintained in a way that fits your lifestyle and budget, such as machine washable versus dry clean. Also, check out the reviews and read comments from others about how easy it is to maintain.
  • Style: Find a style suitable for your room, such as a relaxed look that fits in with a cottage, farmhouse, or casual decor. Or, you can choose something more tailored for a more classic or formal look.
  • Comfort: Comfort is key too, since you’ll likely spend hours on your sofa. Not only does this refer to the material of the slipcover, but also the cushion material, such as down or memory foam.

Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

Which fabric is best for a sofa cover?

The best fabric for a sofa cover depends on your specific needs and preferences, but durable and stain-resistant options like microfiber or tightly woven fabrics like canvas and twill are generally recommended for longevity and easy maintenance. Ultimately, you should consider factors like your lifestyle, budget, and your favorite style when choosing the right fabric for your sofa cover.

Why are slipcovers popular?

Slipcovers remain popular because they offer an easy, cost-effective, and versatile way to update the look of furniture, protect it from stains and wear, and they can be easily removed and cleaned. It makes them a practical choice for households with children or pets.

Who should use slipcovers?

Any person or family can benefit from the use of slipcovers because they offer easier washability. This makes it an ideal choice for any households with pets, kids, or messy people.

How do you clean a slipcovered sofa?

To clean a slipcovered sofa, first, remove the slipcover and check the care instructions on the label. In most cases, you can either machine wash it on a gentle cycle or hand wash it, and then allow it to air dry or tumble dry on a low heat setting.

How long do slipcovers last?

A slipcover’s longevity depends on factors such as the quality of the fabric, how often they’re used, and how well they’re cared for. Generally, a well-made slipcover can last anywhere from a few years to a decade or longer with the proper care and maintenance.
