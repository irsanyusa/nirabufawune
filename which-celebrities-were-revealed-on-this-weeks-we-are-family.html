We Are Family returned on FOX Wednesday night, hosted by Anthony Anderson. Three more celebrities were revealed during the episode, including Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star Kyle Richards.

Kyle Richards, More Celebrities Revealed on We Are Family

The premise of We Are Family is that the non-famous relatives of celebrities perform for the audience in a duet with their famous family member. That family member’s identity is then revealed, similar to The Masked Singer.

During this week’s episode, former NFL wide receiver Terrell Owens was revealed to be the older brother of Sharmaine, as they performed a duet of “24K Magic” by Bruno Mars. Later, Train singer Pat Monahan was revealed to be the father of 11-year-old Rock. They performed “I Don’t Wanna Miss a Thing” by Aerosmith.

Finally, Real Housewives star Kyle Richards was revealed to be the mother of Sophia, as they sang “Love You Like a Love Song” by Selena Gomez. Sophia is the 24-year-old daughter of Richards and her husband, Dancing With the Stars contestant Mauricio Umansky.

Star Player Loses Money by Guessing Paris Hilton

At the end of the episode, star player Tyler had the chance to win up to $100,000 if he correctly guessed who Sophia’s famous relative was. Sophia shared that she’d never performed on such a big stage before, but she was “pushing myself to step out of my comfort zone.”

Sophia and her mystery relative (mom Kyle Richards) performed “Love You Like a Love Song” by Selena Gomez. Clues about Richards’ identity included the Beverly Hills sign and a photo of her Housewives co-star Denise Richards.

Tyler locked in his guess on the very last clue, meaning he would take home $10,000 if he was right. Unfortunately, his guess of Paris Hilton turned out to be wrong, as Richards emerged on stage to perform with her daughter.
